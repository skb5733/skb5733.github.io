Our Story

Drop-in Daycare was founded in 2020 when Sara and Spencer Brown decided to solve a problem they had faced for several years: how to find safe, convenient, on-demand childcare at an affordable price.

Existing options each solved only a part of the problem.

Established childcare centers had set schedules, rigid hours requirements, and were often expensive.

Parents "day out" programs were less expensive, but just as rigid in timing.

Local gyms provided on-demand childcare, but you had to stay on premises and were limited to two hours or less.

Getting a babysitter required advanced notice and offered no guarantee of availability or consistency.

Enter Drop-in Daycare.

We provide a flexible, convenient childcare option for busy parents at consistently affordable prices.
